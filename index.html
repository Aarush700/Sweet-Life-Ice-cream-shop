Our History

Sweet Life was established in 1998 and has been providing the community with the best handcrafted ice cream for over 20 years. We always use the freshest, best-quality ingredients since we are passionate about ice cream and want to give you a flavour you can only get from us.

Types of Ice Cream:

We offer a variety of items to suit everyone, including:

Vanilla

Our vanilla ice cream has a smooth and creamy texture with a rich vanilla flavour since it is produced with fresh cream and actual vanilla beans. Excellent with your prefered dessert!

Chocolate

A traditional taste that everyone adores. Our chocolate ice cream has a rich and luscious flavour since it is produced with fresh cream and premium cocoa powder.

Butterscotch

Your taste buds will be satiated by the classic dessert of butterscotch ice cream, which has a thick, creamy texture. It's a fantastic option for any occasion because it is made with natural ingredients.

Fresh Strawberry

Our delectable strawberry ice cream, crafted with fresh, ripe strawberries, will satisfy your sweet craving.

Rocky Mountain Fudge

Enjoy the luscious and rich flavours of Rocky Mountain Fudge. The ultimate delight for any fudge lover, each mouthful is filled with the ideal balance of creamy richness and chocolatey pleasure. Try it now and you'll enjoy every bite with absolute happiness!

Soft Serve

Serves softly, a deliciously creamy and fluffy delicacy that melts in your tongue is ice cream. Our soft serve has a silky smooth texture and is crafted with the freshest ingredients, which will have you coming back for more. Try it now!
